What is this app?

Thanks for asking! This is an applcication that helps you meet new friends and form study groups here at Boston University!

How does it work?

It's simple! You provide us with some basic contact information, your courses, and when you're free, while we take care of the rest! If we find you a match, we'll send you an email and you can work on your coursework together!

Who made this?

This project was developed by Zach Halvorson and Michael Hendrick in the Fall of 2018. Created with the goal of bringing Boston University students together, WinWin represents what it means to create an inclusive community in the chaotic environment that is college.
