About

This app was developed through BU Spark! Product Innovation Course: CAS CS491 from Fall 2018 to Spring 2019. It matches students with their peers using a modified Gale-Shapley algorithm, which ensures that all students get matched with their best fit.

The current release is a limited version, dedicated just for finals period, to allow us to gain user feedback for the full implementation that will be released in the Fall of 2019. The full-featured app will include the ability to select your free time on a weekly basis, in order to find study groups throughout the whole semester that are more likely to match your schedule.
